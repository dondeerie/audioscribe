# Summary: Seville Arts Interview

## Overview
The interview featured Sigrid Eilertson, an artist affiliated with Seville Arts, providing insights into the artist co-op's structure, offerings, and community involvement.

## About Seville Arts
* Structure and organization: Artist co-op with over 68 artists, 33 of whom are working members.
* Number of artists: Represents various artisans including woodworkers, potters, mosaic artists, glass artists, jewelry artists, textile artists, photographers, and fine artists.
* Business philosophy: Focuses on community involvement, offering affordable art pieces for visitors.

## Artist Information
* Membership details: Artists interested in joining can apply by March 30th through the physical application on seavillearts.org.
* Application process: Emphasizes diversity and inclusion, encouraging skilled artisans to apply.
* Upcoming deadlines: Next deadline for applications is March 30th.
* Artist requirements: Applicants are encouraged to showcase their best work to match the level of skilled artisans at Seville Arts.

## Visitor Information
* What visitors can experience: An array of beautiful art pieces from skilled artisans, with the opportunity to meet and talk to the artists.
* Events and programs: Participates in events like first Fridays, offering a welcoming environment with snacks and beverages.
* Online presence: Developing an online store, expected to be operational by mid-March.
* Pricing and accessibility: Known for being an affordable gallery, providing accessible art options for visitors.
