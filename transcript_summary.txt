1. **Key Points:**
   - Seville Arts is an artist co-op representing over 68 artists, with 33 working members.
   - Inside Seville Arts, visitors will find a wide range of artisans specializing in woodwork, pottery, mosaics, glass art, jewelry, textiles, photography, and fine art.
   - The gallery offers affordable and beautiful art pieces, creating a welcoming and community-based atmosphere.
  
2. **About Seville Arts:**
   - Seville Arts is an artist co-op with over 68 artists, 33 of whom are working members, creating a diverse and inclusive artistic community.
   - The gallery features a variety of artisans specializing in different mediums, providing a unique and affordable art experience for visitors.

3. **Artist Information:**
   - Artists at Seville Arts include skilled artisans in woodwork, pottery, mosaics, glass art, jewelry, textiles, photography, and fine art.
   - The co-op encourages diversity and inclusion in its membership, welcoming artists to apply and showcase their best work.

4. **Visitor Information:**
   - Visitors to Seville Arts can meet and interact with the artists, gaining firsthand insights into their inspirations and processes.
   - Seville Arts is working on launching an online store by mid-March for those interested in viewing and purchasing art remotely.
   - The gallery participates in events like First Fridays, offering a welcoming environment with snacks and beverages for visitors to enjoy while exploring the art.
