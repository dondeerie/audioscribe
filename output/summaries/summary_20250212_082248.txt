# Summary: Seville Arts Interview

## Overview
The interview held on February 12, 2025, featured Sigrid Eilertson, an artist associated with Seville Arts, conversing with host Steve.

## About Seville Arts
* Structure and organization: Artist co-op representing over 68 artists, with 33 as working members.
* Number of artists: 68 artists with 33 active working members.
* Types of art and offerings: Various art forms like woodwork, pottery, mosaics, glasswork, jewelry, textiles, photography, and fine art.
* Business philosophy: Community-based, offering a unique encounter with artists and affordable artwork.

## Artist Information
* Membership details: Open to all, with emphasis on diversity and inclusion.
* Application process: Deadline for the next application is March 30th; application available on seavillearts.org.
* Upcoming deadlines: March 30th for application submission.
* Artist requirements: Emphasis on presenting quality work to match the skill level of existing artisans.

## Visitor Information
* What visitors can experience: Interact with artists, view and purchase various art forms.
* Events and programs: Participation in "First Fridays" with snacks and beverages.
* Online presence: Developing an online store to be operational by mid-March.
* Pricing and accessibility: Proudly advertised as an affordable gallery with a welcoming environment.
